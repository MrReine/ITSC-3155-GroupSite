Our Mission Statement

We are a team of disciplined computer science students committed to developing high-utility software that bridges the gap between complex backend data analysis and intuitive end-user experiences. We value efficiency, transparency through documentation, and data-driven decision-making.

Our primary goal is to deliver a robust ordering and management platform that empowers Managers with trend analysis while providing End Users with a frictionless checkout process. Our strength lies in our ability to prioritize high-impact features—such as Checkout and Menu Management—to ensure a functional and reliable product is delivered.

  • Prioritize Documentation: Every developer must create clean and concise documentation of their codebase so that others can easily and quickly add or change features.
  • Respect Sprint Deadlines: All user stories assigned to a specific Sprint (e.g., Sprint 1 or Sprint 2) must be completed and tested within that timeframe to maintain project momentum.
  • Focus on High-Priority Impact: Team efforts will be focused first on "High" priority features, such as the Checkout system and Menu Management, to ensure the core business goals are met.
  • Data Integrity & Security: When developing data collection and security features, we will prioritize the Manager’s need to monitor trends and resolve security issues quickly.
  • User-Centric Design: All features, from rewards programs to guest checkouts, must be developed with the end user’s convenience in mind to ensure the quickest and easiest ordering experience.
